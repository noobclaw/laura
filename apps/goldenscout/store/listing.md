### Long description (≤4000 chars)
Know your light before you shoot. GoldenScout is a fast, offline planner for
photographers, filmmakers and anyone who chases good light. Pick a spot, pick a
date, and see exactly when — and from which compass direction — the sun and
moon will light your scene.

Everything is computed on your device with deterministic astronomy math. There
is no account, no ads, and no network permission at all — your locations never
leave your phone.

WHAT YOU GET
• Golden hour & blue hour — precise morning and evening start/end times, the
  minutes photographers actually plan around.
• Sunrise & sunset — to the minute, each with the sun's compass bearing so you
  know which way it breaks the horizon.
• Sun & moon compass — a live rose plots the current sun and moon direction and
  altitude; hold your phone up and the rose aligns to where you're pointing.
• Solar noon & peak sun altitude — judge harsh light and shadow length.
• Moon phase & illumination — plus moonrise and moonset (Pro).
• Any location — use your current GPS position or save your favorite shooting
  spots by name.
• Any date (Pro) — scrub forward or back to plan a shoot days or months ahead.

WHY GOLDENSCOUT
Big planners are powerful but heavy and pricey. GoldenScout is focused and
friendly: open it, read your light, go shoot. One clean screen, no clutter, no
subscription.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
GoldenScout ships without the INTERNET permission. The sun and moon almanac is
pure on-device calculation; GPS and the compass are local sensors. Nothing is
uploaded, tracked, or shared. Times are shown in your device's timezone.

FREE VS PRO
Free: today's light for your current location.
Pro (one-time $3.99): plan any date, save unlimited shooting spots, and full
moon details. No subscription — buy it once, keep it.

Plan the light. Catch the moment.
