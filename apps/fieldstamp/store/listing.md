## English

**Title (≤30 chars)**
FieldStamp GPS Camera

**Short description (≤80 chars)**
Burn GPS, altitude, bearing & time into every photo. Fully offline. Buy once.

**Full description**
FieldStamp is a fully offline GPS field-evidence camera. The moment you tap the shutter, it burns the GPS coordinates, altitude, compass bearing, time, and project name directly into the photo's pixels — not added afterward, but composited at capture, so the image itself is a piece of archivable field evidence.

Whether you're doing site inspections, surveying, insurance claims, property maintenance, or field documentation, one tap captures the location and timestamp with the photo — no more scribbling down "where and when was this taken."

■ Why FieldStamp
• What you shoot is what you get: coordinates, bearing and time are burned in at capture — evidence you can trust
• Truly offline: the app requests no network permission, so your photos and coordinates never leave your phone (verifiable at review)
• No subscription: a one-time purchase — unlike other field cameras that charge monthly
• Clean and fast: a tidy Material 3 UI with dark mode; open and shoot
• Privacy first: no account, no ad SDKs, no data collection

■ Core features
• Live info band: see current GPS, altitude, bearing, time and accuracy (±m) right in the viewfinder
• File photos by project / work order in a clean gallery
• Multi-select to export PDF inspection reports and CSV ledgers
• Share the stamped photos directly
• Coordinates in decimal or degrees-minutes-seconds (DMS); altitude in meters or feet

■ Who it's for
Field engineers and surveyors, insurance adjusters, property and facility maintenance, security and law-enforcement documentation — anyone who needs photos tagged with location and time.

The free version takes fully stamped photos; unlock Pro for multiple projects, PDF/CSV export, DMS coordinates, and to remove the small on-photo tag.
