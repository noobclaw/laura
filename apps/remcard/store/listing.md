## English

**Title (≤30 chars)**
Remcard - Offline Flashcards

**Short description (≤80 chars)**
Spaced-repetition flashcards, fully offline. SM-2 scheduling, no subscription.

**Full description**
Remcard is a fully offline spaced-repetition flashcard app. It uses the classic SM-2 algorithm to schedule each card right when you're about to forget it — so you remember more with fewer reviews. All of it runs on your device: no network, no uploads, no account.

Whether you're learning vocabulary, cramming for an exam, memorizing medical terms, or picking up a new language, just build a deck, add cards, and let the algorithm do the rest.

■ Why Remcard
• Proven method: classic SM-2 spaced repetition — correct answers push the next review further out, misses come back soon
• Truly offline: the app requests no network permission, so your cards never leave your phone (verifiable at review)
• No subscription: a one-time purchase unlocks unlimited decks — no monthly fees
• Clean & modern: Material 3 UI with dark mode, ready to use, no learning curve
• Privacy first: no account, no ad SDKs, no data collection

■ Core features
• Multiple decks, each showing how many cards are due today
• Front/back cards you can add, edit, and delete anytime
• Review sessions: tap to reveal the answer, grade with four buttons (Again / Hard / Good / Easy) to auto-schedule the next review
• Everything stored locally — instant and private

■ Who it's for
Students preparing for exams, language learners, professionals with lots of terminology to memorize, and anyone who dislikes subscriptions and values privacy.

The free version includes 2 decks and unlimited cards; unlock Pro for unlimited decks.
